Support

Need help with SMART trip - Community App (SMART Train Schedule)? We are happy to help troubleshoot issues, answer questions, and collect feedback.

Contact Support

Email: smart-train@proton.me

If you are reporting a bug, please include:

  • Your device model (for example, iPhone 15 / iPad Air)
  • Your iOS/iPadOS version
  • The app version (for example, 1.2.1)
  • Approximate time the problem occurred
  • A short description and screenshot (if available)

What This App Can Do (Current Features)

  • Real-time trip status on each trip card, including delay, cancellation, and skipped-stop indicators (when available)
  • In-app service alerts with an unread badge and a slide-in alerts panel
  • Interactive route planning: choose departure and arrival stations and swap them easily
  • Ferry connections: see connecting ferry schedules when traveling to Larkspur
  • Weekday / weekend schedules: toggle schedule types
  • Next-train highlighting: quickly spot the next available train
  • Dark mode: matches system theme (with manual override)
  • Accessibility: designed for screen readers and keyboard navigation
  • Bilingual support: full English + Spanish interface

Data Sources and Accuracy

This is a community-built app. Schedules and real-time indicators depend on the underlying transit feeds, which can occasionally be delayed or incomplete.

If you see something that looks wrong (missing trip, incorrect delay badge, and similar issues), email support with:

  • station pair (from -> to)
  • the trip time you expected
  • a screenshot (if possible)

Common Questions

Is this the official SMART app?

No. SMART trip is not affiliated with Sonoma-Marin Area Rail Transit (SMART). It is a community project.

Why does a trip not show a delay or cancellation?

Real-time info may not be available for every trip at all times. When the feed does not provide it, the app falls back to scheduled times.

I got an alert badge. Where do I read it?

Open the Service Alerts panel in the app to view unread alerts. Once viewed, the badge count updates.

Can you add a feature?

Yes. Email a short request. If it is a bug or issue, include the details listed in the Contact Support section above.
